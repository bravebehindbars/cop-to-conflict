Welcome To My Webpage

Hello! I'm Dan. I'm currently incarcerated in Maine, serving a 3 year sentence. I am a father to a wonderful daughter.

Before my incarceration, I worked as a police officer and sheriff's deputy for about 7 years. Prior to that I was a military police soldier in the U.S. Army for 6 years. I believe I have a unique perspective, having been on both sides of the system. I hope to be able to advocate for change in our broken criminal justice system using that perspective, and help other people who may be going down the same road I was before they end up like me. I want to take this negative situation, and make something positive out of it.


Note:

This website is a project I created for the Brave Behind Bars Computer Science Program I am attending while incarcerated. Thank you to the folks over there for this opportunity and all of your help! (I ended up with the best TA!) I plan to update this site and be more active with it upon my release in the spring of 2025. This is also an extremely watered-down version, as I was told I cannot have my name, name of facilities I was at, agencies I worked for, or other "personally identifiable information" on this site. I apologize if some of the site seems vague, it's not by choice and I will be able to be more transparent once I'm released.
